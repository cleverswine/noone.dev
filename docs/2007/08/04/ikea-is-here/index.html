Ikea is Here

August 4, 2007

The Portland Ikea finally opened last week. We went and we shopped, but not on opening day. We actually didn’t buy all that much. I got a new desk chair that is very mod looking. The couch that we’ve been wanting for months has been disconinued and they had nothing else close enough to it. That was quite disappointing. For now we’re stuck with my $80 Goodwill couch from the 70s.
