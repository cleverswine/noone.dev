Wii Finally Got a Wii

May 18, 2007

After months of looking and waiting, Bren came across a Nintendo Wii yesterday! I guess Nintendo finally caught up with the demand. The last gaming console that I’ve owned was an Intellivision II, back in the 80s. I’ve been waiting for the right system to come along, as I’m not impressed (or good at) the modern genre of games. So far the Wii has not disappointed. We only have Wii Sports, which came bundled, but it will take a while to get tired of that. My favorite game so far is Tennis. The Wii will also help with my diet because it’s interactive.
