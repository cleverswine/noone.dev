Jul 26 2007 Oregon Brewers Festival

It’s that time of the year again. The summer Oregon Brewers Festival is going on this weekend. I haven’t even looked at the beer list, but I walked over there to eat lunch today and checked it out. I know that I want Piny the Elder. We are all going tonight (Thursday) for a change, because it gets way too crowded on the weekend.
