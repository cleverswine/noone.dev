Jul 29 2007 Musicals Galore

We saw Hairspray last night, which is the movie version of the Broadway musical which is based on a movie by John Waters. I was pleased to see a cameo by John Waters at the beginning, as I am a fan of his. The movie was pretty good, and does the original justice. If you can tolerate a musical I’d recommend it.

Tonight we are going to see Mama Mia, which is a musical play featuring music from Abba. I happen to like Abba, otherwise I’d be hesitant to see something of this sort.
