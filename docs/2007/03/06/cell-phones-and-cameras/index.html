Mar 06 2007 Cell Phones and Cameras

I’ve never wanted a cell phone with a camera on it because I’m all about separation of functionality. I want a camera that works well, a phone that works well, a calculator that works well, etc etc. When you start combining unrelated functionality into one “gadget”, you lose quality in the individual pieces. However, these days it’s nearly impossible to get a decent phone without a camera built in, so now I have one. I disposed of my old Nokia and got the Motorolla Razr. I have to admit, it’s kind of neat having a 1.3 megapixel camera tucked away in my tiny phone. Some photos from my phone are on Flickr.
