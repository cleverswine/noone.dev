Buffy is Back

March 14, 2007

After seven fabulous seasons of Buffy the Vampire Slayer, writer Joss Whedon has begun work on season eight – in comic book form. The comic is being published by local company Dark Horse Comics. I have already pre-ordered the first 3 issues, plus issue #1 with an alternate cover. I’d rather continue to see Buffy as a television show, but a comic will do.

Update, March 16: Buffy: Season 8 #1 sells out – gets second printing!
