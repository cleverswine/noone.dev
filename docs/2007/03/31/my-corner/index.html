My Corner

March 31, 2007

We did some bedroom rearranging and I created a masculine corner for myself. I included part of my Van Gogh collection, my retro gold chair (or “baby puke green”, as some would say), my Ikea dresser (inherited from an ex of an ex), and my Ikea nightstand. It’s very simple and “in place”. The other corner is girly, but nice.
