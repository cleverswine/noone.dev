Thinking About Thinking

February 21, 2008

I’ve been working unusually hard lately, and by “working” I mean sitting in front of computers solving complex problems. Coincidentally, I’ve lost a couple of pounds this week. Now I’m wondering if thinking burns calories. Am I burning calories by thinking about the possibility of thinking burning calories? The brain is an amazing machine! Now, if only I could upgrade my processor and memory, and rewire a few circuits.
