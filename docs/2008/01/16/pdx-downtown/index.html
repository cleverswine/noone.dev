PDX Downtown

January 16, 2008

I had a brilliant idea. Since I work in downtown Portland, and frequently take walks around the city, I thought I’d take photos of interesting things and post them here. I was going to start today during lunch, but it’s way too friggin’ cold out there. I had to speed walk back to the heated office. I did get a decent shot of the parking garage that I park in. Woo hoo.
