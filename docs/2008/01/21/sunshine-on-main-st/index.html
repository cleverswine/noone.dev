Jan 21 2008 Sunshine on Main St.

It’s a warm and sunny day here in Portland. Not. It’s actually a freezing and sunny day over here. I just walked to get lunch and I don’t know if I’ve ever been so cold! I think it’s about 30+ degrees, but the wind is blowing hard. If it’s going to be this cold, we should at least get snow out of it! We get enough sunshine in the summer.
