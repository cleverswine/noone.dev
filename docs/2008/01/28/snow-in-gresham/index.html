Jan 28 2008 Snow in Gresham

We got a bit of snow last night in Gresham. I’d say there was about 1.5″ when I left for work this morning, and I think it’s supposed to snow some more throughout the day. In downtown Portland, there’s pretty much nothing. I should have worked from home today so I could have played in the snow.
