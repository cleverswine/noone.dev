Mar 20 2008 Check Out

I would not normally advocate Wal-Mart, as they are evil in so many ways, but I’ve been reading the Wal-Mart blog lately. It’s called Check Out. I don’t know exactly who the “associates” are that write on this blog, but it does bring a human side to the company. Obviously, it’s a marketing ploy, but I still enjoy reading it. It is G rated and censored (no foul language allowed), like the store. I wonder if they will address their unfair business practices that bring other companies to their knees? I doubt it.
